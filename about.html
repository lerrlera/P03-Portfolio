Nice to Meet You!

Hello, I'm Valeriya, a passionate UX Designer on a quest to create impactful and empathetic digital experiences. Currently, I'm studying at SFU's Interactive Arts and Technology program. Throughout my journey, I've explored a wide range of disciplines, from psychology to computer science, human-computer interaction, and interactive systems. Now, I'm focused on merging these diverse skills into a holistic approach to design.

My goal is simple: to use design as a tool for promoting change and fostering genuine connections. I believe that by combining my knowledge and expertise, I can create experiences that resonate with users on a deeper level.
